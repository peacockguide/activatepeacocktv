How to Install the Peacock TV App
---------------------------------

Before activating Peacock TV, you need to install the application on your device.

1. Turn on your smart TV or streaming device.
2. Open the device’s app store or marketplace.
3. Use the search option and type **Peacock TV**.
4. Select the official Peacock TV application.
5. Click **Download** or **Install**.
6. Wait for the installation to complete.
7. Open the app after installation.

Once the application opens, you will see options to sign in or activate your account.

How to Activate Peacock TV Using an Activation Code
---------------------------------------------------

Activating Peacock TV is simple and takes only a few minutes.

Follow these steps carefully:

1. Open the Peacock TV app on your streaming device.
2. Select **Sign In** or **Activate Device**.
3. An activation code will appear on your TV screen.
4. Open a web browser on your phone, tablet, or computer.
5. Visit the Peacock activation page.
6. Sign in with your Peacock account email and password.
7. Enter the activation code shown on your TV.
8. Click **Continue** or **Activate**.

After a few seconds, the Peacock TV app will refresh and your device will be activated successfully.

You can now browse content and start streaming your favorite shows and movies.
